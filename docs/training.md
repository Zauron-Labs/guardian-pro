# Training & Onboarding

Guardian Pro is designed for minimal training requirements, with most radiologists familiar with PACS-based workflows. We provide comprehensive training resources to ensure smooth adoption and optimal utilization.

To get acquainted with the system, [click here to see a sandbox version of the software](http://20.65.224.246/expo).

- **On-Demand Video Tutorials**: Step-by-step guidance with sample cases and workflows
- **Custom Video Creation**: If you have specific questions not covered in our existing videos, we'll create custom content for your needs
- **1-on-1 Sessions**: Personalized training through screenshare sessions or virtual calls with our expert team

## Training Timeline

Our structured 3-week onboarding process ensures you get up and running quickly while maintaining quality and compliance:

### Week 1: Quality Champion Setup
- **Focus**: Critical miss handling and quality assurance workflows
- **Process**: When a critical miss occurs, the designated Quality Champion receives an email notification containing:
  - Accession number for the study
  - Direct live link to access the study
- **Actions**: Utilize existing institutional policies and procedures, which may include:
  - Direct patient contact
  - Consultation with the original radiologist
  - Data aggregation for future feedback and quality improvement

### Weeks 2-3: User Training
- **Focus**: General user adoption and workflow familiarization
- **Rationale**: Most users require minimal training as they are already experienced with:
  - Reading studies on PACS systems
  - Managing email communications
  - Standard radiology workflow processes
